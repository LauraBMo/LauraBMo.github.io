Research Interests
Algebraic Geometry: Hilbert schemes, linear systems, Waring type problems and tensor decomposition.

PhD project
I study the parameter space for clusters of sections of a family. In particular, I am adapting Kleiman's iterated blow ups to parametise clusters of sections. It led's to a notion, the blow up sections family, which generalises blow ups and combines properties from the flattening stratification, universal sections family and, of course, the blow up.
