PhD project (Poster)
I study the parameter space for clusters of sections of a family. In particular, I adpeted Kleiman's construction of iterated blow ups to parametise clusters of sections. This led us to the notion of Universal scheme of cluster of r sections. We show that, as iterated blow ups, such schemes can be construct iterativly, but now the iterative step is not as simple as blowing up a regularly embedded diagonal of a sutible product. In order to formalise this iterative step we define and study the blow up split sections family, a generalitation of blow ups, which combines properties from the flattening stratification, universal sections family and, of course, the blow up.
