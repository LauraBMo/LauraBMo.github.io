I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Joaquim Roé and supported by Departament de Matemàtiques de la UAB, PIF.
Currently I am visiting for ten weeks Professor Prof. Dr. Michael Joswig and Bernd Sturmfels at Institut für Mathematik der Technischen Universität Berlin in the group member of Fachgebiet Diskrete Mathematik/Geometrie.

My research interests are mostly in Algebraic Geometry. In particular, I study the geometry of a locally closed subfunctor of the Hilbert functor aiming to obtain sharper upper bounds on the dimension of linear systems of curves on surfaces.



PhD project (Poster)

I study the parameter space for clusters of sections of a family. I generelised Kleiman's construction of iterated blow ups to paramertise clusters of sections, which leads us to the notion of Universal scheme of cluster of r sections. We show that, as iterated blow ups, such schemes can be constructed iteratively, but now the iterative step is not as simple as blowing up a regularly embedded diagonal of a suitable product. In order to formalise this iterative step, we define and study the blow up split sections family, a generalisation of blow ups, which exhibits properties of flattening stratifications, universal sections families and, of course, blow ups.
